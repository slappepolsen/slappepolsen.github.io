App

SP Workshop

I made one of my childhood dreams come true: I made a free and open-source app! SP Workshop helps you easily sub the scenes of your favorite ship. You don't need to know complex tools or the command line. Just install it, open it, and click buttons.

  • Pull subtitles out of video files and translate them into another language
  • Burn-in subtitles back into the video so they permanently show on screen
  • Download episodes in batches from streaming sources (works for Carlice, Roxara, and way more)
  • Auto-transcription, that is, make subtitles from audio when there are none

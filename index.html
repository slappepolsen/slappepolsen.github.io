App

SP Workshop

I made one of my childhood dreams come true: I made a free and open-source app! SP Workshop helps you work with subtitles for video you’re entitled to use — locally, on your own machine. You don't need to know complex tools or the command line. Just install it, open it, and click buttons.

  • Pull subtitles out of video files and translate them into another language
  • Burn-in subtitles back into the video so they permanently show on screen
  • Batch workflows for managing episodes and downloads where the tool supports it — only for sources and files you may legally access (details on GitHub)
  • Auto-transcription to draft subtitles from audio when none exist
